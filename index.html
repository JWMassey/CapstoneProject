DEMO

Select Your Class:

Class cannot be changed later. Subclasses can be chosen later, but also cannot be changed. (Subclasses are not yet implemented into this demo)
  • High HP and Physical stats
  • Low Speed and Magic stats
  • Subclasses:
    • Duelist - Focuses on high single target damage with powerful single strike moves.
    • Knight - High defenses and HP, made to keep yourself alive.
    • Guardian - High defenses and Taunt effects to make enemies target you over allies.
  • High Speed and Medium Attack stats
  • Lower HP and Defense stats
  • Subclasses:
    • Marksman - Lands devastating physical blows to quickly defeat a single target.
    • Shadow - Utilizes enchantment and debuffing magic to cripple foes. Status effects are a Shadow's specialty.
    • Beast Tamer - Calls upon beastly companions to assist you in combat. While not the strongest alone, its strength comes from its allies
  • High Magic and medium Speed stats
  • Lower HP and Physical stats
  • Subclasses:
    • Purified - A dedicated healer, learning many spells to heal yourself and allies, as well as protection magic.
    • Pestilent - Combination of damage over time and summoning, harnesses necrotic and distortion magic to devastate foes.
    • Warlock - Utilizes a combination of spells of the major elements to fit any role.
